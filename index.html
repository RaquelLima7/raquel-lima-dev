Quick Order

Onebitcode's Hackathon winning project.

Quick Order is a system that helps restaurants manage their orders. There are three users: admin, cook, and clerk. In the admin profile, there is a dashboard where you can filter the information of the orders made by days as well as check the situations of queued, progress, completed, canceled, and total. The admin will also be able to view, and modify other information such as categories, products, users, kitchen view and orders. In the cook profile, you have access to orders and can make changes as the progress changes. In the clerk profile, you can make new ones, view all of them, and see your orders.

Technologies: Ruby on Rails Bootstrap PostgreSQL Rspec(TDD)
